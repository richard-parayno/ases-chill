HOW IT ALL STARTED

In December 1994, Big Chill was established as a new concept, serving premium quality blended shakes made with 100% fresh-cut fruit. It caters to a diverse age group within the AB & upper C segments of the market with branches conveniently located in shopping malls, universities & office buildings within Greater anila. Its extensive network of shops & kiosks provide convenient access to fresh fruit shakes anytime, anywhere.

Big Chill is known for its award-winning quality of beverages, having been recognized as the "Best Fruit Shake Drink" by the Product Quality Excellence Awards for both the regional and national categories.

Fresh Bar is an expanded concept of Big Chill which offers the same fresh fruit shakes along with a line of hearty gourmet soups, healthy pasta offerings, fresh salads and sandwiches. It aimts to make healthy meal options readility available tothe same market that appreciates Big Chill's selection of fresh fruit shakes.

Big Chill and Fresh Bar is an exciting foodservice beverage concept ideally suited to today's healthy and fast paced lifestyle.
